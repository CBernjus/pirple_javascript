TODO List App

With this app you can manage your day to day life.

You can organize your upcoming tasks in different lists.
Add as many tasks as you want and if you're done with them mark them as completed.
When there are too many completed lists you can tidy up your list in one 'click'.

Add, rename and delete the lists as you want.

Change any of your user data at any time.
If you desire you even can delete your account.
